    The University of Balochistan was established in October-1970 through an Ordinance issued by the then Governor Balochistan. In June 1996, the Balochistan Assembly passed an ACT called the University of Balochistan ACT 1996, later-on the provincial assembly had passed a unified ACT in 2022 for all public sector Universities.
