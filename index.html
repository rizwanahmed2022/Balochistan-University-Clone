ABOUT UNIVERSITY

Our Education System Inspires You More.

The University of Balochistan, established in October 1970, has a rich heritage of providing quality higher education across diverse fields of Science, Arts, and Humanities. Governed by the University Act of 2022, the institution encompasses 6 faculties, 52 departments, specialized centers, affiliated colleges, and campuses dedicated to fostering academic growth and research excellence.

Comprehensive Education Services

Offering diverse programs and student support services to nurture talent and promote lifelong learning.

Global Academic Partnerships

Collaborating with international universities to enhance research opportunities and cultural exchange.
